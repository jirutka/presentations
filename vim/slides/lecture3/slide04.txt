
Folding
-------
▶   :set foldmethod
        manual
        indent
        expr
        marker
        syntax
        diff   (diff mode)
▶ Commands
    zf  (fold)  - only for foldmethod=manual
        - works like a regular operator
    zo  (open)  zO - recursive variant
    zc  (close) zC - recursive variant
    zv  (view cursor line)
    zd  (delete)   - not for text
    :set foldclose=all
    :set foldopen=all
