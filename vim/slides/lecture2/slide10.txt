
Registers
---------
    ◆ 0-9   automatically filled by yank and delete operators
            editation history, very useful in some situations
    ◆ =     eval mode pseudoregister
            wait for user input, you can use expression that
            is immediatelly evaluated:
            Normal mode:
            "=6*7<cr>p
            Insert mode:
            Ctrl+R=6*7<cr>
    :reg - shows all regs
